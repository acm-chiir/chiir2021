Student Grants

The SIGIR Students Travel Grant program will cover the cost of registration for any student author presenting a full, perspective, short, demo, resources, or DC paper at CHIIR 2021.

To be eligible, the student must:

  1. be an author or co-author of a paper;
  2. be the one presenting the work at the conference; and
  3. complete the following application form: https://unc.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_eKA63O0WdHuCGYR?conference=CHIIR2021

IMPORTANT: The application form will expire on 31 December 2020 (AOE).
