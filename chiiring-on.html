CHIIRing each other on

We know in these difficult times it can be hard to make time to write, so we are offering a CHIIRing on service. This is solidarity, not mentorship, and designed to encourage all of us to set aside time to write.

We will be hosting two Zoom writing sessions per week at on Tuesday at 11am AEST/6PM Monday PST/9PM Monday EDT/2AM BST/9AM CST and 2PM EDT/11AM PST/7PM BST/4AM AEST/2AM CST. These are times where you can join a Zoom and write in silence for an hour, and the Zoom will remain open for another hour afterwards for chat.

We are also offering a find-a-buddy service through Twitter. This is not to find a co-author or mentor, but rather a way to find someone who you can set up your own Zoom writing sessions with. To use this service, please tweet what time (and timezone) you would like to write in with the hashtag #chiirmeon, and we will retweet this through the CHIIR official account and our accounts.
