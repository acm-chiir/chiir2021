CHIIR 2021

6th ACM SIGIR Conference on
Human Information Interaction and Retrieval
Online, 15-19 March 2021

Welcome to the sixth ACM SIGIR Conference on Human Information Interaction and Retrieval (CHIIR) (pronounced “cheer”), which will take place on 15–19 March, 2021 (online). CHIIR provides a forum for the dissemination and discussion of research on the user-centered aspects of information interaction and information retrieval. CHIIR focuses on elements such as human involvement in search activities, and information seeking and use in context. The conference represents a merger of two successful past events: the Information Interaction in Context conference (IIiX) and the Human Computer Information Retrieval symposium (HCIR).

Latest

UPDATE: Due to the continued uncertainty with travel caused by the COVID-19 virus, the sixth ACM SIGIR Conference on Human Information Interaction and Retrieval (CHIIR) (pronounced “cheer”) will be held online 15-19 March, 2021. This means there will be no requirement for authors or other participants to travel to present their work.
We welcome your submissions of full or short papers, position papers, demonstrations, and resources for the technical programme, and workshops or tutorials to run in conjunction with CHIIR.
The full CHIIR Organising Committee is up and running.
