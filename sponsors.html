Sponsorships

CHIIR 2021 welcomes expressions of interest from potential sponsors.

CHIIR offers a unique opportunity to connect with scientists developing next-generation techniques at the intersection of search, information interaction design, machine learning, and artificial intelligence; and to learn about the state-of-the art and future research directions. In addition, CHIIR offers the chance to meet and recruit the top PhD students. It is an excellent venue to promote your organisation's brand to thought leaders with global influence. Sponsors will receive extensive exposure to this community during the conference promotion and during the conference. We look forward to welcoming you to the virtual conference in 2021 and encourage you to consider the benefits of sponsoring CHIIR 2021.

More information can be found in the sponsorship brief. Feel free to get in touch with the sponsorships chair with any questions or further information.
